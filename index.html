About me

I am an undergraduate student at Minnesota State University, Mankato, majoring in Biomedical Sciences with a focus on research in soil microbiology. My academic interests center around exploring microbial interactions in soil ecosystems and their implications for environmental sustainability and human health. Through my research, I am developing a deeper understanding of microbial communities and their potential applications in biotechnology and medicine. I am committed to advancing knowledge in the biomedical sciences, with the goal of integrating microbiology and sustainability into innovative solutions for global health challenges.
